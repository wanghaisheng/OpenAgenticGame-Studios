
Games meet a high standard of quality


Ensure that the game is engaging and has good player retention. Make the game's hook fun and provide enough content to encourage players to return.
Make it easy for players to enter the game loop. Use short tutorials, intuitive controls, and provide objectives that are clear and/or clearly explained.
The game should be a complete product when released on GD, with the primary goal of providing enjoyment to players. Do not use prototype games that redirect players to other websites.
Providing an English version of the game is mandatory. Additional languages are optional, and will broaden the game's appeal.


 Top-quality in-game visuals

Ensure that the visuals are harmonious, including the art-style and colour scheme.
Avoid visual clutter that might overwhelm the player. Be mindful of the amount of the visuals special effects, UI elements, and animation present in the game.
Maintain consistency in resolution throughout the game. All elements should be designed to fit within a single resolution.


Modern design in line with audience expectations


In-game graphics are a crucial component of game design. Character designs, backgrounds, and other graphic details should be visually appealing to captivate and engage players.
Consider the age and preferences of the target audience when selecting the colors and sizes of graphic elements. For example, choose larger UI elements and a more contrasting color palette for an older audience.
Ensure that the UI elements are visually attractive and user-friendly to your target audience. They should be immediately understandable and easy to find.
Pay attention to cross-cultural communication. The game mechanics should be clear regardless of cultural background. Avoid elements that may confuse or alienate players.



Please make sure your game does not include any of the following, as they are strictly prohibited by GD:
Implementation of ads other than the ones coming from GD’s SDK
Tracking and collection of users’ personal data
Use of intellectual properties (IPs) without proper ownership rights. Proof of ownership must be available.
Use of any outgoing links, including redirection, Google Analytics, other analytics, social media links, etc.
Excessive branding and logo usage. Branding should be used moderately and in designated areas such as the credit pages, loading screen, and main menu.
Placing contact details or website URLs within the game itself.
Explicit use of alcohol, drugs, sex, politics, or offensive religious statements or topics.

5. HIGH-QUALITY GAME PAGE FILLING
The recommended title length is no more than 20 characters. Titles exceeding this limit will result in a header spanning two lines, with any excess replaced by dots. Titles over 40 characters are not permitted.
Limit the use of tags to a maximum of 5. Please select the ones most relevant to your game.
Ensure that the game description is between 100 and 500 words in length.
Please select all applicable languages. An option to specify 'No language' is available, but must be manually selected.
