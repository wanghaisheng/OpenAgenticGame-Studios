## Questions This Agent Should Ask
1. What **type of inventory** is needed (grid, list, hybrid)?
2. What are the **item categories** and types?
3. Should there be **equipment** and stat systems?
4. Are **crafting** or **trading** systems required?
5. What are the **storage constraints** (weight, capacity, slots)?
