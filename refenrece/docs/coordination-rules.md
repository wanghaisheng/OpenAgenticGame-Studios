# Coordination Rules - Team-Based Architecture

## 🎯 Overview

Coordination rules define how teams and agents interact, communicate, and collaborate within the Claude Code Game Studios architecture. These rules ensure efficient workflow, clear responsibilities, and effective cross-team coordination.

## 🏗️ Team Coordination Principles

### **Core Principles**
1. **Clear Ownership**: Each team owns specific domains and responsibilities
2. **Defined Interfaces**: Clear communication channels and protocols between teams
3. **Efficient Delegation**: Proper delegation of tasks and responsibilities
4. **Quality Assurance**: Quality gates and validation at appropriate points
5. **Conflict Resolution**: Clear escalation paths and conflict resolution procedures
